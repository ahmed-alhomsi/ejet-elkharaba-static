Just because we are in Lebanon, we feel the pain of the random electrecity outage

Regardless of what power resources you are using, you may feel the pain:

Battery Drain:

when you continue to consume too much and the electricity is suddenly off

Lost Opportunity:

when you electricity becomes available at night and you forgot to turn on Battery charger

Waste of Time:

when you need to perform some tasks based on your anticipation of the electricity schedule

Lack of Saving:

when you need to save money by consuming on the grid-electricity more than the other power sources
